In Memoriam
SAD Hatter Book Club
i
Here lies
Josh
Attended twice. Left forever.
Deloitte took him from us.
He came. He read. He billed.
ii
He Came for
Mr. Ripley
He showed up for a book about a charming fraudster who abandons everyone around him.

We should have seen the signs.
Art imitates life. Life imitates Deloitte.
iii
He Also Came for
My Friends
A book about friends who drift apart and leave each other behind.

He then proceeded to drift apart and leave us behind.

The timing was not lost on us.
He read the book. He became the plot.
